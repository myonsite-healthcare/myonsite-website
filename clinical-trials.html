Frequently Asked Questions

How do you ensure participant compliance and retention in home-based collections? +
We employ multiple strategies: personalized scheduling to participant preferences, automated reminders, dedicated patient advocates, and real-time tracking of appointment completion. Our 72% higher retention rate reflects our commitment to participant satisfaction and study protocol adherence.
What regulatory approvals do you have for decentralized trials? +
We operate under FDA decentralized trial guidance (2023), maintain GCP certification, hold CLIA lab accreditation, CAP audits, and NAACLS standards compliance. All our phlebotomists are nationally certified, and we maintain 100% chain-of-custody compliance with full audit trails.
Can you handle complex specimen requirements (plasma, serum, specialized tubes)? +
Absolutely. We collect whole blood, plasma, serum, urine, saliva, and specialized specimens. Our team receives protocol-specific training for each trial, and we maintain proper handling, temperature control, and documentation for all specimen types.
What's the typical turnaround time for results and reporting? +
We maintain a 24-hour SLA for specimen processing and results delivery. Our real-time e-Manifest system provides digital chain-of-custody documentation, and our analytics dashboard gives sponsors real-time visibility into trial metrics, retention, and protocol compliance.
How do you maintain data security and HIPAA compliance? +
All data is stored in HIPAA-compliant cloud infrastructure with end-to-end encryption. We maintain role-based access controls, audit logging, and regular security assessments. Our e-Manifest system is designed with privacy-by-design principles.
