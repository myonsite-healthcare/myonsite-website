What We Offer

Comprehensive Mobile Trial Services

Mobile Specimen Collection

Protocol-compliant blood, urine, tissue, and biospecimen collection at participant homes, reducing screen failure and improving retention rates.

Cold Chain Management

Validated temperature-controlled transport from collection to central lab delivery, with continuous monitoring and documentation for every sample.

Protocol Adherence

Strict adherence to sponsor and IRB-approved protocols, SOPs, and ICH-GCP guidelines. All staff trained in GCP and protocol-specific requirements.

Chain of Custody

Complete electronic chain of custody documentation using validated eTMF-compliant systems, ensuring audit-readiness at every step.

Kit Preparation & Handling

Sponsor kit unpacking, labeling, inventory management, and proper storage at mobile sites. Kits shipped to our coordinators before study visits.

Participant Engagement

Compassionate, professionally trained coordinators who build trust with participants, maximizing retention and protocol compliance throughout the trial.
