Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services, scheduling, and more.

📋 Before Your Draw

This depends on the specific lab tests you're having. Many tests don't require fasting, but some do (typically 8-12 hours). Your doctor or our team will let you know beforehand if fasting is necessary for your specific tests.

Stay hydrated, eat a light meal if not fasting, and have your doctor's order or insurance information ready. Wear comfortable, loose-fitting clothing with sleeves that roll up easily. Try to relax and avoid stressful situations before your appointment, as stress can affect some test results.

Our certified phlebotomists are compassionate and experienced in working with anxious patients. Let them know about your concerns when you book—they can help you relax and make the experience as comfortable as possible. Deep breathing, looking away, or distraction techniques can help too.

Most appointments take 10-15 minutes. This includes check-in, the draw itself (usually 3-5 minutes), and brief aftercare instructions. Our phlebotomists are highly skilled and efficient, ensuring you're in and out quickly.

Bring a valid photo ID, your insurance card (if you have one), and your doctor's lab order. You can also bring a light snack or beverage to enjoy after your draw. Have your phone ready for any last-minute confirmation calls.

🔒 Results & Privacy

Most results are available within 24-48 hours. Some tests may take longer depending on complexity. You'll receive results through your patient portal, email, or phone, depending on your preference.

Results are delivered securely through encrypted patient portals, by email, or by phone. We use HIPAA-compliant systems to protect your privacy. You can also request results be sent to your healthcare provider.

Yes, we are fully HIPAA compliant. Your medical information is protected under strict federal privacy regulations. We encrypt all data, limit access to authorized personnel only, and maintain comprehensive security protocols.

Only you, your authorized healthcare providers, and authorized laboratory personnel can access your results. We never share your information without explicit consent, except as required by law.

We collect basic demographic information, insurance details, and medical history relevant to your lab tests. All information is handled with strict confidentiality and stored securely.

🔬 Clinical Trials

We provide phlebotomy services for clinical research studies, including participant screening, blood collection, sample processing, and secure documentation. Our team is experienced in managing the precise protocols that trials require.

Eligibility depends on the specific trial. Contact us at (941) 271-0701 or visit our clinical trials page to learn about current opportunities and enrollment requirements in your area.

Participants may gain access to new treatments, receive free lab work and medical monitoring, and contribute to scientific advancement. Some trials offer compensation for time and travel.

All clinical trials are conducted under strict ethical oversight and regulatory approval. Participants receive informed consent documentation and can withdraw at any time without penalty.
