Mobile Phlebotomy for Florida Families and Seniors

myOnsite Healthcare specializes in bringing professional phlebotomy services to Florida's unique population — from Miami's diverse communities to Tampa's bustling metro to the retirement havens of Naples, Boca Raton, and Sarasota. Florida's large elderly population, with more than 21% of residents aged 65 and older, demands convenient healthcare solutions that respect the active lifestyle retirees have earned. Whether you're a seasonal snowbird managing ongoing health conditions, a senior living in a retirement community seeking preventative care, or a Latin American family prioritizing regular wellness checks, our certified phlebotomists eliminate the need for clinic visits in Florida's heat and traffic. We serve Miami, Orlando, Jacksonville, Fort Lauderdale, Clearwater, West Palm Beach, and dozens of other Florida cities with same-day appointments, insurance acceptance through Florida Blue, Molina, Sunshine Health, Medicare, and others. Our service supports chronic disease management for diabetes and heart disease, senior wellness programs, clinical trial participation, and community health initiatives across Florida's most active regions.
