Frequently Asked Questions

How do I schedule a blood draw? +
Simply call us at (941) 271-0701 or use our online booking system. Choose your preferred date, time, and location. We'll send a certified phlebotomist to you within 24 hours in most cases.
What if I'm nervous about needles? +
Our phlebotomists are trained to work with anxious patients. We use gentle techniques, thin-gauge needles, and can provide a comfortable environment at your home or workplace. Many patients find our service less stressful than clinic visits.
Do you accept insurance? +
Yes! We work with most major insurance plans. Out-of-pocket costs typically range from $10-40 depending on your plan. We can verify your coverage before your appointment.
How long does a blood draw take? +
Most appointments take 10-15 minutes from start to finish. This includes check-in, the actual draw, and labeling. We come to you, so there's no waiting room time.
When will I get my results? +
We guarantee results within 24 hours of specimen collection. Most results are available same-day. Results are sent directly to your physician and you can request a copy for your records.
