myOnsite Healthcare Extended Hours: Now Serving Patients More Conveniently Than Ever

December 20, 2023 3 min read myOnsite Healthcare Team

We've heard your feedback, and we're excited to announce that myOnsite Healthcare has expanded our service hours to make mobile phlebotomy even more accessible for patients across all 7 states we serve.

Our New Extended Hours

Effective immediately, our mobile phlebotomists are available:

  • Monday – Friday: 7:00 AM – 7:00 PM (local time in each service area)
  • Saturday: 8:00 AM – 4:00 PM (most service areas)

This expansion means working professionals can now schedule early morning draws before heading to the office, families can book weekend appointments without disrupting the work week, and elderly patients who prefer morning appointments have more options than ever.

Why We Expanded Our Hours

The most common piece of feedback we received from patients was that scheduling during standard 9-5 hours was difficult for those with full-time jobs. Many patients were having to take time off work for what is often a 15-minute blood draw — completely eliminating the convenience advantage of mobile phlebotomy.

Quick Fact: Over 60% of our new patient bookings since expanding hours have been for early morning (7-9 AM) or late afternoon (5-7 PM) slots — confirming that extended availability was the right decision for our community.

Same-Day Appointments Available

We also want to highlight that same-day appointments remain available in most of our service areas. If your physician sends in a lab order this morning, we may be able to have a phlebotomist at your door this afternoon. Call us at 1 (888) 566-9990 to check availability in your area.

What Hasn't Changed

Our commitment to clinical excellence, patient dignity, HIPAA compliance, and certified professional standards hasn't changed one bit. The same high-quality service you've come to expect from myOnsite Healthcare — just with more convenient scheduling options to fit your life.
