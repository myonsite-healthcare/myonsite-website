We're Hiring

Certified Phlebotomist

📍 Multiple States ⏱️ Full/Part-time

Join our mobile phlebotomy team and collect specimens from patients in their homes and workplaces. You'll be the face of myOnsite Healthcare — bringing compassionate, professional care directly to patients.

Key Requirements

  • Active CPT (Certified Phlebotomy Technician) or ASCP certification
  • Clean driving record & reliable vehicle
  • Excellent communication & customer service skills
  • Ability to work independently across multiple locations
  • HIPAA compliance & confidentiality awareness

Lead Phlebotomist / Team Lead

📍 Regional ⏱️ Full-time

Lead a team of phlebotomists across a region. Manage schedules, ensure quality compliance, mentor team members, and maintain the highest standards of patient care and specimen handling.

Key Requirements

  • 2+ years phlebotomy experience (1+ in leadership preferred)
  • CPT or ASCP certification required
  • Strong team management & communication skills
  • Knowledge of CLIA, HIPAA, and compliance standards
  • Problem-solving & conflict resolution abilities

Clinical Operations Coordinator

📍 Headquarters ⏱️ Full-time

Support our clinical operations team. Schedule collections, manage specimen tracking, coordinate with CROs and sponsors, and ensure seamless execution of mobile phlebotomy services across all states.

Key Requirements

  • Experience in clinical operations or healthcare coordination
  • Excellent organizational & time management skills
  • Proficiency with scheduling software & data management
  • HIPAA certification preferred
  • Attention to detail & quality assurance mindset

Business Development Representative

📍 Remote / Travel ⏱️ Full-time

Build relationships with CROs, sponsors, and healthcare systems. Present myOnsite's decentralized trial solutions, identify partnership opportunities, and drive revenue growth in clinical trials segment.

Key Requirements

  • 2+ years B2B sales or business development experience
  • Knowledge of clinical trials or healthcare industry preferred
  • Excellent communication & relationship-building skills
  • Self-motivated with ability to hit targets
  • Comfort with travel (20-30% estimated)
