Our Origin Story

myOnsite Healthcare was founded on a simple yet powerful belief: geography should never determine healthcare quality. We recognized a critical gap in the healthcare system—millions of patients faced unnecessary barriers to essential lab testing, whether due to mobility challenges, rural locations, or busy schedules.

Our founder saw firsthand how patients struggled to access timely, professional phlebotomy services. That inspired us to build a mobile solution that brings certified phlebotomists directly to patients' homes, offices, and facilities. Today, we've transformed healthcare accessibility for over 50,000 patients across 7 states, and we're just getting started.
