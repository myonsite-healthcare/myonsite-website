Our Story

Redefining Healthcare Delivery Since 2016

myOnsite Healthcare was founded with one powerful idea: healthcare should come to you. We recognized that millions of patients — elderly, homebound, immunocompromised, or simply time-constrained — struggled to access basic laboratory services.

Our certified mobile phlebotomists and clinical specialists now serve patients across 7 states, partnering with leading health systems, clinical research organizations, and individual patients to deliver laboratory excellence at the doorstep.

From routine blood draws to complex clinical trial specimen management, we handle it all — with the professionalism, compassion, and reliability that our 50,000+ patients have come to trust.

What Drives Us

Our Mission, Vision & Values

Our Mission

To make high-quality laboratory and clinical services accessible to every patient — at home, at work, or wherever life takes them — through compassionate, professional mobile healthcare delivery.

Our Vision

To be the nation's most trusted mobile healthcare partner — setting the gold standard for on-site specimen collection, clinical trial support, and patient-centered care across the United States.

Our Values

Compassion, integrity, clinical excellence, and patient dignity guide everything we do. We treat every patient like family and every specimen with the precision that accurate diagnosis demands.
